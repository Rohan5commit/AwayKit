# Privacy Story

## Why Local-First Matters for Matchday Fans

Football fans going to matches often face:

- **Poor connectivity** — Stadiums and transport hubs have overloaded networks
- **Privacy concerns** — Group chat content, location, and spending habits are sensitive
- **Trust issues** — Who holds the shared money? What if the organizer disappears?

## AwayKit's Three Privacy Guarantees

### 1. AI Runs Locally (QVAC)

Traditional apps send your questions to cloud AI providers. AwayKit runs inference on your device. Your translations, venue questions, and trip summaries never leave your phone.

### 2. Chat Syncs Peer-to-Peer (Pears)

Traditional chat apps store messages on central servers. AwayKit uses Pears to sync directly between group members' devices. No server sees your conversations.

### 3. Payments Are Self-Custodial (WDK)

Traditional split-pay apps require a trusted platform. AwayKit uses self-custodial USDt wallets. You control your funds. Settlements happen directly between wallets.

## The Offline Advantage

When 50,000 fans enter a stadium, cell networks collapse. AwayKit still works:

- QVAC AI continues on-device
- Pears syncs locally between nearby peers
- Wallet state is cached locally

This isn't just a privacy feature — it's a product advantage.
